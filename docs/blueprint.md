# **App Name**: Kentekrown

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Filtering: Browse a curated collection of African-inspired streetwear and accessories, with categories and filters for easy navigation.
- Detailed Product View: View high-resolution product images, comprehensive descriptions detailing cultural significance and materials, available sizes, and pricing.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add, remove, and update quantities of desired items before proceeding to checkout.
- Secure Checkout Process: A streamlined and secure process to complete purchases, including shipping and payment options.
- User Account & Order History: Allow users to create and manage an account, view past orders, and track current purchases.
- AI Styling Assistant: A generative AI tool that recommends outfit combinations or styling tips based on specific Kente patterns, personal style input, or occasions, empowering users to 'wear their heritage' with modern flair.
- Cultural Story Hub: Each product links to its unique cultural narrative or inspiration, celebrating the 'real patterns, real stories' behind the designs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary: A rich, warm gold (#FFCA72) that evokes wealth and the vibrant energy found in African textiles and royalty.
- Background: A deep, desaturated earthy brown (#27221A), creating a sophisticated and grounding dark canvas.
- Accent: A vibrant, fiery crimson-red (#F72C0D) for calls to action and to highlight passion and resilience within the brand's spirit.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif), offering personality, aligning with fashion and art for a bold and stylized header presence.
- Body text font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif), providing an elegant, intellectual feel for rich cultural narratives and product descriptions.
- Icons will blend contemporary flat design with subtle nods to geometric Kente patterns or traditional African motifs, maintaining cultural pride.
- A clean, modern, and spacious grid-based layout for product displays, with dynamic hero sections and immersive content blocks to highlight cultural stories.
- Subtle, fluid animations for product card interactions, smooth page transitions, and elegant reveal effects for detailed content, enhancing the luxurious and engaging user experience.
